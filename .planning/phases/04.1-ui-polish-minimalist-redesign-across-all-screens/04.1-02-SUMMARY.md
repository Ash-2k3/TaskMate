## Decisions Made

- Removed centering wrapper from form screens: forms should fill the viewport like TodayView, not be constrained to 480px
- Flat rows for ReflectionsHistory: consistent with the TaskRow pattern established in Phase 2; accordion separator via border-b is sufficient
- focus-visible over focus: keyboard-nav-only ring is the shadcn standard and avoids the always-visible ring on mouse click
